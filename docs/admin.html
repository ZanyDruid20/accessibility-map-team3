🦮 About Us 🦮


This is a student made Accessibility Routing Application for our very own UMBC Campus!
Brought to you by: Andrew Lam, Furnom Dam, Kaila Hoskins, and Sanaa Thomas


Our goal was to create an application that is able to help students, faculty, or any person of any status to be able to navigate their way across campus! With this, we wanted to focus specifically on accessible routes on campus, which can be difficult to find since our campus is built on the side of a hill, but they exists! We hope that our application is able to provide you a quick and accessible way to your destination! Go Retrievers! :)
